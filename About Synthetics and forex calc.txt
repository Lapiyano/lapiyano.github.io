Synthetics & Forex Calculator
About this app
The Synthetics & Forex Calculator app is designed for traders who require precision in their trading calculations. Access real-time prices for various symbols, including metals like Gold and stocks like US30. Our app offers a range of tools to help you make informed trading decisions.
Key Features:

1. Real-Time Calculations: Access up-to-date prices for symbols such as Gold and US30.

2. Stop Loss & Take Profit Calculator: Utilize six different modes to manage your trades effectively.

3. Pip Calculator: Input point values from mobile and desktop for accurate risk and profit calculations.

4. Pivot Point Calculators: Includes methods like Floor, Woodie, Camarilla, Demark, and Fibonacci for varied trading strategies.

5. Commission Calculator: Tailored for traders using zero spread accounts, ensuring accurate cost calculations.

6. Custom Asset Settings: Input specifications such as swap rates, spread, leverage, and contract sizes for more personalized calculations.

7. Leverage Calculator: Provides accurate margin cost estimations to support your trading strategy.

The Synthetics & Forex Calculator is your essential tool for precise and reliable trading calculations, helping you stay ahead in the market.
